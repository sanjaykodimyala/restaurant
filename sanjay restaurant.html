Sanjay Restaurant

Sanjay Restaurant is a delightful dining destination that caters to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food lovers. With a diverse menu featuring a mix of Indian and international cuisines, it offers something for everyone. The restaurant boasts a warm and inviting ambiance, making it an ideal spot for family gatherings, casual outings, or even a quiet meal alone. Known for its flavorful dishes and attentive service, Sanjay Restaurant ensures a memorable dining experience for all its guests. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty vegetarian curry or a succulent non-vegetarian delicacy, this place has you covered!

veg

Starters: Crispy vegetable spring rolls, paneer tikka, and vegetable samosas.

Main Courses: A variety of curries like paneer butter masala, mixed vegetable curry, and dal makhani, served with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

South Indian Delights: Dosas, idlis, and uttapams with flavorful chutneys and sambar.

Chinese Favorites: Veg fried rice, Hakka noodles, and Manchurian.

Snacks: Chaat items like pani puri, bhel puri, and sev puri.

Desserts: Gulab jamun, rasmalai, and ice cream.

Non veg

Starters: Chicken tikka, mutton seekh kebabs, and crispy fried prawns.

Main Courses: Butter chicken, mutton rogan josh, chicken biryani, and fish curry, served with naan, paratha, or steamed rice.

Grilled Specialties: Tandoori chicken, grilled fish, and spicy lamb chops.
